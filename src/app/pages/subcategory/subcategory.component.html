We provide comprehensive equipment for situation centers

As a rule, a modern situation center is a software and multimedia complex, which includes display surfaces (large-scale video walls, LCD panels, etc.), many operator workstations, a powerful server for storing the software necessary for the operation of the situation center provision, concentration of operational data, analytical tools.

The same applies to data transmission means, switching, and control facilities for the nodes of the Situation Center (SC), from video cameras in monitored areas to generating reports for the ultimate data recipient. In short, a situational center is a classical comprehensive multimedia project consisting of several main high-tech components. The main task of a situational center is to monitor the state of an object according to specified parameters, eliminate non-standard situations, prevent emergencies, and ultimately enhance the efficiency of object management. The situational center should allow for predicting scenario developments and formulating optimal decisions related to the functioning of the object.
